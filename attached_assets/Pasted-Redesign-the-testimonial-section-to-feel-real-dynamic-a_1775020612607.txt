Redesign the testimonial section to feel real, dynamic, and high-trust (modern SaaS style).

1. Layout (CRITICAL)
- Replace static grid with a continuously flowing layout
- Create 2 horizontal rows of testimonial cards
  - Row 1 scrolls left
  - Row 2 scrolls right
- Infinite seamless loop (duplicate items for continuity)
- Slow scrolling speed (must be readable)
- Pause on hover

2. Number of testimonials
- Minimum 20 items (prefer 20–24)
- Vary content length (1 line, 2 lines, 3 lines mixed)
- Avoid identical card sizes (slight variation allowed)

3. Content tone (VERY IMPORTANT)
- Make testimonials feel natural and human
- Avoid polished marketing language
- Slightly imperfect, conversational tone

Examples:
- "Didn’t expect this to be this useful."
- "Search alone saves us hours every week."
- "We replaced 2 tools after switching."
- "Honestly way faster than our old workflow."

4. Name style (TRUST CORE)
- Use realistic human names (IMPORTANT)
- Mix formats:
  - Full name: Minsoo Choi
  - Initial: J. Kim
  - First name only: David
- Mix global names (Korean + English)

5. Company / role style (ANTI-FAKE)
- DO NOT use fake company names (e.g. CreativeCo ❌)
- Use anonymized format:
  - "N사", "D2C brand", "글로벌 커머스", "SaaS startup"
- Focus on role context instead of company identity

Examples:
- Growth PM, N사
- Marketer, D2C brand
- Founder, SaaS startup
- Creative Director, 글로벌 커머스

6. Visual design
- Keep cards clean and minimal
- Slight variation in card width
- Reduce overly large padding
- Use soft shadow + subtle border
- Add gradient fade on left/right edges of container (important for flow effect)

7. Extra trust signals (OPTIONAL but recommended)
- Add small tags inside cards:
  - "YouTube"
  - "Ads"
  - "TikTok"
  - "Ecommerce"
- OR subtle category labels

8. Goal (IMPORTANT)
- Must feel alive and continuously updating
- Must NOT feel like “3 fake testimonials placed in a row”
- Should resemble modern SaaS landing pages (Stripe / Notion style)

Final Result:
A dynamic, infinite testimonial flow that feels real, diverse, and trustworthy.
