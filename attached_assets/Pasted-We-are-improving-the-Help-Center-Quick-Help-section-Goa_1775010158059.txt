We are improving the Help Center "Quick Help" section.

Goal:
Make it action-oriented, easy to click, and optimized for fast problem resolution.

---

[1. Overall Structure]

- Keep the current card-based layout (6 cards)
- This section is NOT informational, it must drive users to solutions
- Each card must link to a detailed help page

---

[2. Card Interaction]

- The entire card must be clickable (not just text or icon)
- Add cursor: pointer
- Add hover interaction:
  - Slight background highlight
  - Subtle border or shadow increase
- Ensure clear visual feedback on hover

---

[3. Text Style (IMPORTANT)]

Unify all card text into a question format.

Replace existing text with:

- Can't find your file?
- Upload not working?
- Video stuck?
- Out of credits?
- Billing problem?
- Invite your team

Tone:
- Short
- Clear
- Conversational
- Problem-focused

---

[4. Card Order (Priority-based)]

Reorder cards based on most common user issues:

1. Upload not working?
2. Video stuck?
3. Can't find your file?
4. Out of credits?
5. Billing problem?
6. Invite your team

---

[5. Icon Usage]

- Icons must represent meaning, not decoration
- Use intuitive icons:
  - File / search → file issues
  - Upload → upload issues
  - Video → processing issues
  - Credit card → billing
  - Users → team

- Ensure icon + text together clearly communicate the problem

---

[6. Section Title]

Change:
"Quick help"

To one of:
- "Quick fixes" (preferred)
- "Get help fast"

---

[7. UX Principles]

- This is NOT a documentation list
- This is a problem-solving entry point
- Users should feel:
  → "I click this and solve my problem immediately"

---

[8. Future Consideration (Do not implement yet, but keep in mind)]

- Cards should later be dynamically sorted based on real usage data
- (Most clicked / most searched issues first)

---

Final Goal:
Transform this section from a simple list of issues into a high-conversion problem-solving gateway.
